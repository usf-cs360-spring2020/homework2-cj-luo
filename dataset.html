About Dataset

DataSet

Visualize the “Baseline Cross-Sectional Estimates of Child and Parent Income Distributions by College” dataset from the “Mobility Report Cards: The Role of Colleges in Intergenerational Mobility” project from Opportunity Insights.

  • About

    As part of our research studies, we put out publicly available datasets that can be used by other researchers and practitioners to support their own work. These datasets allow you to analyze social mobility and a variety of other outcomes from life expectancy to patent rates by neighborhood, college, parental income level, and racial background. You can search for datasets by geographic level (e.g., Census tracts), by topic (e.g., education), or by the title of the paper.

  • Data size

    There are 2202 rows and 34 columns data. Each row is a college info.

  • Column Description

    1. super_opeid: A unique 9-digit number assigned by the 911 Dispatch Center (DEM) to this call. These number are used for both Police and Fire calls.
    2. name: Name of college (or college group).
    3. tier: Selectivity and type combination (see Table 6 for more detailed descriptions of these groups):
    4. par_q[PARQUINT]:Fraction of parents in an income quintile [PARQUINT]. 1 is the bottom quintile and 5 is the top.
